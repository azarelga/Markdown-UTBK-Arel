> $$W =m(C_{p}-C_{v})\triangle T$$

Rumus diatas didapatkan dari
$W = P(V_{2}-V_{1})$
$W = PV_{2}-PV_{1}$

Karena $PV = nRT$, maka
$W = PV_{2}-PV_{1} = nR(T_{2}-T_{1})$

Karena $nR = m(C_{p}- C_{v})$, maka
$W = m(C_{p}- C_{v}) . T_{2}-T_{1}$
$W = m(C_{p}- C_{v}) . \triangle T$
